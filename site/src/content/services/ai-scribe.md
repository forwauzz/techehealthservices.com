qa:
  - question: What is AI scribe?
    answer: >-
      AI scribe is an advanced digital tool designed to automate time-consuming
      tasks such as data entry and note-taking. Using artificial intelligence,
      it captures and summarizes spoken conversations with patients into
      detailed and clinically relevant medical notes.
  - question: How does AI scribe work?
    answer: >-
      AI scribe employs speech-to-text and AI technologies to transcribe
      physician-patient interactions into meaningful content. This includes
      summarizing patient visit notes and performing administrative tasks like
      generating referral letters. Key benefits include reducing the burden of
      administrative tasks, increasing direct interaction time with patients,
      enhancing patient engagement, and improving documentation accuracy.
  - question: AI scribe VS dictation software?
    answer: >-
      Unlike traditional dictation software, AI scribe goes beyond converting
      speech to text. It can extract information from transcriptions and
      integrate it into specific fields within electronic medical records
      (EMRs). Additionally, it can access patient charts, set reminders, and
      facilitate orders for tests or prescriptions through voice commands.
  - question: Is AI scribe being implemented?
    answer: >-
      Evaluation studies of AI scribe are currently underway to assess its
      effectiveness in reducing administrative workload for healthcare
      providers. These studies, involving diverse groups of primary care
      providers, aim to showcase how this technology can streamline healthcare
      operations and enhance patient care.
